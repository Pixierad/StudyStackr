Privacy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

StudyStackr is an independent schoolwork planner for tasks, subjects, study sessions, timers, friends, chats, and profile settings.

StudyStackr is not Google, Microsoft, your school, or any other education provider. Only use your StudyStackr account details here. Do not enter passwords from other services.

Information You Provide

Depending on which features you use, StudyStackr may store:

  • Your email address for sign-in and account security.
  • Your username, display name, avatar, banner, and profile details.
  • Your tasks, subjects, deadlines, study sessions, timer names, and study totals.
  • Friend requests, friend connections, chat rooms, and messages.
  • Theme choices and other app settings.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide the app features you choose to use, keep your account available across devices, show your profile to accepted friends, and help keep the service working correctly.

Account And Cloud Storage

When cloud sync is enabled, account and app data may be stored using Supabase. The website may also use local browser storage so the app can remember settings and keep working smoothly on your device.

Friends And Profiles

People you add as friends may be able to see profile information you choose to show, including your display name, username, avatar, banner, account age text, and study heatmap information.

Messages

Chat messages are used to provide the chat feature. Do not share private passwords, financial information, or sensitive personal information in chats.

Third-Party Services

StudyStackr may rely on hosting, database, authentication, and analytics providers to operate the app. These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service.

Your Choices

You can edit profile details, delete tasks or study sessions, and sign out from inside the app. For account help or removal requests, use the contact page.

Contact

For privacy questions or account help, contact mrdogguy@outlook.com.
